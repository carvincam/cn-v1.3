Contractions Now!
 About
Contractions Now! provides a simple solution to keep track of your contractions and intervals before going into labor. Press the 'START' button on the home screen to initiate a contraction. Press 'STOP' to begin the interval timer. The timer turns red during a contraction, and green during an interval. Press the 'RESET' button to clear all data.

When the time between contractions reach 5 minutes or less apart, that's typically the time to start heading to the hospital. A notification will be sent to your phone's tray when the app detects this. While this is a widely accepted reccomendation, you should always consult with your physician for medical guidance.
